Receipt Field Guide
Every verification returns these fields — here's what they mean.
claim_idUnique fingerprint for this check. Your retrievable proof of due diligence.
verified_atExact UTC timestamp the live government source was queried — not a cached result.
sourceAuthoritative government list checked. OFAC SDN = US Treasury. BIS DPL = Dept. of Commerce.
source_urlDirect link to the government database. Examiners can independently verify the source.
resultMATCH or NO_MATCH against this source at this exact moment in time.
match_score100% = exact. 85–99% = fuzzy — catches misspellings and name variations.
content_hashSHA-256 of the list version checked. Proves exactly which version was in use.
